This world had fighting arenas, and by extension, they had to have combat tournaments for those who made a living with their proverbial fists to decide who stood at the top. I had no concept of saving my money. Not when I planned to break free of this world.

All in all, it cost me less than two gold coins. Impulse was a bit expensive, but the others were of limited utility and consequently cheap.

I’d have appreciated it if she’d stopped giving me that look every time money came into the picture.

I took my change of silver coins and the dimensional magic gems, which emanated a strange and curious hue.
