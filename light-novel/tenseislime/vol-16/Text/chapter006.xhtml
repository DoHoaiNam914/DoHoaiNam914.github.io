He was right.

This really sucks.

Geld the Barrier Lord…and Kumara the Chimera Lord? Wait, Kumara’s a cryptid, isn’t she? She’s not descended from Ivalage, the World-Destroyer Dragon, is she? Nah, no way…

Pfft… I really hate working…
