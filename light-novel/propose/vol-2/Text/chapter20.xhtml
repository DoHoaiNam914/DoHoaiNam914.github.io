The students from the Tower—sitting throughout the training hall—every single one of them rose to their feet, letting out a terrible roar.

Then, activating their various magical abilities, they started lashing out at the Garden’s students.

“What…?!”

“Kyargh!”

A whirlwind of dismay and alarm, a storm of screams and cries filled the training hall.

Then, in a droll, obsequious tone, Silvelle announced:

“And so, farewell—as of today, the Garden is finished.”
