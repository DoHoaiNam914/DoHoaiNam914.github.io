Prologue: Vow

I hate it.

This world.

Everything in it.

I hate it all.

That’s why I decided...why I made a vow.

On that day, I realized that kindness was just an ideal.

It was the moment I lost everything without any reason.

I was betrayed and had everything snatched away from me.

So, I made a vow to end this world, riddled with foolish humans.

To take vengeance.

That was something I was capable of.

I had the right to do it.

I had the vexing power to make it possible.

That’s why I’ll destroy it all.

There’s no worth in a world like this.

The die has already been cast by none other than my own hand.

That’s why I couldn’t turn back anymore.

I had no intention of turning back anyway.

I cannot forgive this world. These humans.

And more than anything, I couldn’t forgive myself.

That’s why I desperately...

Charged towards ruin.

That was my punishment.

For not being able to save someone...
